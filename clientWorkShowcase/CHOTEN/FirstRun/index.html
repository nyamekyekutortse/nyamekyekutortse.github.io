About Us

CHOTEN Limited is an electro-mechanical engineering company in Ghana which is in the business of providing qualit, high-end electrical services, building accessories, elevators and measuring tools. At CHOTEN our business services extends to but not limited to procurement of general engineering products, servicing and installation of elevators, generators, electricals, air conditioners and more, ducting and thermal installation included as well as the supply of vehicles of various brands, makes and models.

At CHOTEN, we maintain a world class standard of efficiency in accordance with the most high end technologies in conformity with international standards of safety, quality and security.

With our wide range of services and expertise, CHOTEN Limited extends its services to all sectors nationwide; Government, Private, Industrial, International, Education, Cultural, Residential and Housing sectors.

 

OUR VISION

To be the most responsible, respectable and most sought after engineering firm that places its clients at the forefront of business.

OUR MISSION

To execute projects at unmatched standard of quality, provide safe working conditions and deliver quality work within reasonable time frames
