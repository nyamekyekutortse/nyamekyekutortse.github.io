    Working Process

    Ideally, construction workers would fully understand the ramifications of inadequate safety precautions and thus the act in a manner to ensure site-wide well being -but knowing that this not a perfect world, CAL Construction makes sure that on. every project site, there are safety officers to ensure safety work processes are adhere to.

    Employee Safety

    Creating Safety awareness in the lives of our employee is a core mandate on us at Construction Ambassadors. No matter the qualification or role of our employee we understand that people make mistake and that endangers other lives during working process.
